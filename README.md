A **end-to-end Power BI project** built entirely from raw data — from messy Excel sheets to a professional executive dashboard.

## 🔄 Full Data Pipeline (My Work)
- **Received** raw Meta Ads billing reports (monthly) + booking sheets
- **Cleaned & transformed** data in **Power Query** — removed duplicates, fixed formats, merged tables
- **Built data model** from scratch — relationships, calculated columns
- **Wrote DAX measures** — ROAS, CPL, Lead-to-Sale %, Total Revenue, YoY calculations
- **Designed full dashboard UI** in Power BI with custom dark theme

## 📊 Dashboard Highlights
- **Rs 2 Billion+** revenue tracked across 5 brands
- **5,011 leads** from Meta Ads analyzed
- **132x ROAS** — Rs141 earned per Rs1 spent
- **160 closed units** · 3.2% lead-to-sale conversion rate

## 📋 Strategic Recommendations By Mubinah Baiyg

Based on the data analysis, here are my recommendations for Kings Group:

### ✅ Increase Budget
- **Kings Real Estate** — Highest revenue (51.38%) and strongest ROAS.
  Best performing page. Scale ad spend immediately.

### 🎯 Improve Targeting
- **Vistro Real Estate** — Good revenue share (24.86%) but CPL is high.
  Needs better audience targeting to reduce cost per lead.
- **Mahar Real Estate** — Moderate performance.
  Refine targeting to improve lead quality and conversion rate.

### ⚠️ Reduce Budget
- **Inaya Estate** — Low revenue contribution (4%).
  Reduce spend until targeting and creative are improved.

### ❌ Consider Stopping
- **Zavia** — Lowest revenue (1.33%) with minimal returns.
  Pause campaigns and reassess strategy before spending more.
